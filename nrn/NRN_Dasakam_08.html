Dasakam – 8

Deluge and from that raises a lotus and from that Lord Brahma is born

-------------------------------------------

Jagad srusti praakara Varnana

Maha vishnu blesses Brahma and He starts the creation - In between pralayam is called Naimeetika pralaya - between 1000 yugas, He does his duty of creation. When such creation happens, what happened, Battatri explains. Every creature assimlates within Lord Narayana. Wake me up after one kalpam, Lord Narayana says. So, after we wakes up, the bud sprouts comes from His navel and Lord Brahma comes for starting Creation
